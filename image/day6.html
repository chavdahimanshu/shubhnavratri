WELCOME TO SHUBH NAVRATRI DAY 6

ABOUT

Goddess Katyayani

On the sixth day of Navratri, the worship is dedicated to Goddess Katyayani. Goddess Katyayani is one of the forms of Goddess Durga and is considered one of the most powerful forms of the goddess. She is depicted as having four arms, holding a sword in one hand and a lotus in the other. She rides a lion, symbolizing courage and strength.

Goddess Katyayani is worshipped on the sixth day of Navratri to seek her blessings for strength, courage, and victory over obstacles. Devotees believe that her worship can help remove hurdles and difficulties from their lives and grant them the strength to face challenges.

SINGER : FALGUNI PATHAK

Falguni Pathak is a popular Gujarati Female Singer. She is known for Gujarati Bhajans, Songs, Arti, Stage Performances, and Garba Nights. Popularly, She is also known as Queen of Dandiya of Gujarat.

She is popular for her Stage Performances and Graba Nights. Kinjal Dave made her singing debut with the song – Char Char Bangadi Wali Gadi. Her first album released in 1998 – Kya Mil Gayi Dusri. She has also got awards for her singing in india.

She has released more than 200 music album.

Traditational Cloths : Cloths color is Yellow

Day 6 of Navratri is associated with the color Yellow. The color yellow represents brightness and energy. It symbolizes the color of the harvest and is often associated with positivity and knowledge.Many people choose to wear yellow-colored clothing on this day to celebrate and worship the goddess Durga or her various manifestations.

It's important to note that these color traditions might vary, and some regions or communities may follow different color sequences or have their own unique customs regarding clothing during Navratri.
