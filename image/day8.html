ABOUT

GODDESS MAHAGAURI

On the eighth day of Navratri, Goddess Durga is worshiped in the form of Goddess Mahagauri. Mahagauri translates to the one who is extremely white, symbolizing purity and serenity. She is often depicted as having a fair complexion, wearing white clothes, and riding a bull.

Goddess Mahagauri is believed to have the power to fulfill all the desires of her devotees. Worshipping her is said to bring peace, prosperity, and fulfillment of wishes. Devotees seek her blessings for self-purification, mental peace, and spiritual progress.
